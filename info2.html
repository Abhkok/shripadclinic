About

Dr. Namrata Kokare is a distinguished general physician and surgeon with expertise in Ayurveda and Panchakarma, a holistic healing approach rooted in ancient Indian medicine. Her practice not only encompasses traditional medical expertise but also integrates the principles of Garbha Sanskara, which focuses on nurturing the physical and mental well-being of expectant mothers and their unborn children. Dr. Kokare is also a proponent of yoga, using its physical and meditative practices to enhance overall health and promote a balanced lifestyle. Her multifaceted approach to health care combines modern medical practices with time-honored holistic treatments, offering a comprehensive path to wellness for her patients.
